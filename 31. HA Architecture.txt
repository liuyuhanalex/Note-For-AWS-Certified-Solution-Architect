HA Architecture
Plan For Failure: Everythins fails. Everything!

Remember the following:
1. Always Design for failure
2. Use Multiple AZ's and Multiple Regions where ever you can
3. Know the difference between Multi-AZ and Read Replicas for RDS.
4. Know the difference between scaling out and scaling up.
5. Read the question carefully and always consider the cost element.
6. Know the different S3 storage classes.
