Elastic Transcoder
What is Elastic Transcoder?
1. Media Transcoder in the cloud
2. Convert media files from their original source format into different formats that will play on smartphones, tablets PCs,etc.
3. Provides transcoding presets for popular output formats, which means that you don't need to guess about which settings work best on particular devices.
4. Pay based on the minutes that you transcode and the resolution at which you transcode.
